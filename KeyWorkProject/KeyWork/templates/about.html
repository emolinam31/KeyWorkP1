KeyWork Vision

KeyWork is a service designed to address the needs of operational employers who manage large volumes of physical resumes that are often lost or mishandled during the application process, as well as individuals who need to create or digitize their resumes. KeyWork is a web application that streamlines the digitization of physical resumes through designated collection points, where applicants can drop off their printed CVs to be scanned and converted into digital formats. Additionally, the platform offers an innovative parallel solution: generating structured digital resumes via short interviews conducted directly at these collection points. Unlike traditional methods or competing services, KeyWork seamlessly integrates physical and digital resume management, featuring voice input capabilities for effortless digital conversion, ensuring that no CV is overlooked. Develop an efficient solution to digitize physical resumes through accessible collection points and a tool that enables the creation of digital resumes via recordings that can be uploaded. Our goal is to optimize operational talent management, ensuring no profile is lost and enhancing the experience for both candidates and companies.

Our Mission

At KeyWork, we believe that finding the right job should be simple, efficient, and effective. Our mission is to streamline the job search process by providing an innovative CV upload system that connects candidates with employers looking for exactly their skills and experience.

How It Works

Using cutting-edge technology, KeyWork allows you to upload your CV in multiple formats - as a document, an image, or even a voice recording. Our intelligent system analyzes your qualifications and matches you with the perfect opportunities.
